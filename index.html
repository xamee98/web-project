About Us

About Rainbow High Unversity

The Rainbow High University, in conformity with the vision of the university envisions “to be a dynamic, innovative and renowned center of excellence in learning, research, training and consultancy with emphasis on national relevance and international recognition in the different disciplines of every study field”. Its mission is “to promote learning, research and training among all members of the university, industry and community and to help individuals achieve their full potential through development of the whole person with a view to making significant contributions to the regional, national and global needs”.
